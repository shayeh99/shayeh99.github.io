Steamed Bao Dough

    2 C all-purpose flour
    1 tsp instant yeast
    1 tsp baking powder
    2 tsp sugar
    1/8 tsp salt
    3/4 C milk, cold
    vegetable oil for brushing
  1. Place the flour, instant yeast, sugar, salt in a mixing bowl. If you are using a stand mixer, use a dough hook attachment. Add cold milk or room temperature water. Add cooking oil. You may need to add more milk/water if the dough is still a bit dry. Add more teaspoon by teaspoon until the dough comes together and continue to knead for 5 minutes.
  2. Then add in the salt and continue to knead until the dough is smooth again
  3. Wrap the dough with a plastic wrap and let it rest for 15 minutes before shaping
  4. Divide the dough into 10-12 equal portion (depends on how small or big you want the steamed buns to be)
